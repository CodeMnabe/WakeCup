Our Journey

The year was 1980 when WakeCup was imagined after experiencing some of the finest coffees on an educational trip to Brazil and the South Americas. We wanted to break the monotony of low profile, non-descript coffee that was doing rounds in those cafeterias of the 80s. But life threw us in a different direction, and we ended up in the tech industry with a desire to get into the world of coffee someday. In 2005, that dream was brought to fruition when we quit our jobs and started WakeCup.

Our Passion

At WakeCup, our passion for coffee is matched only by our passion for our customers. We are driven by the pursuit of perfection in every cup we serve. From sourcing the highest quality beans to perfecting our brewing techniques, we strive to offer an unparalleled coffee experience. Our baristas are trained extensively to bring out the unique flavors of each blend, ensuring that every sip you take is a celebration of our craft.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainability is at the heart of WakeCup. We believe in responsible sourcing and work directly with farmers who practice sustainable agriculture. Our commitment extends beyond just coffee beans; we ensure that all our packaging is eco-friendly and our operations minimize waste. By choosing WakeCup, you are supporting a company that values the planet and strives to make a positive impact on the environment.

Community Engagement

WakeCup is more than just a coffee company; it’s a community. We host regular workshops and events to bring coffee lovers together, sharing knowledge and fostering a sense of camaraderie. Our café spaces are designed to be welcoming hubs where people can relax, work, and connect. We also partner with local organizations to support community initiatives, reinforcing our commitment to giving back to the communities we serve.

Innovation in Coffee

Innovation drives us forward at WakeCup. We are constantly exploring new ways to enhance the coffee experience, from experimenting with new brewing methods to developing unique coffee blends. Our research and development team works tirelessly to stay ahead of trends and bring you exciting new products. Whether it’s a new flavor profile or a cutting-edge piece of brewing equipment, you can always expect something fresh and exciting from WakeCup.

Recognitions and Awards

Our dedication to excellence has earned us numerous accolades over the years. WakeCup has been recognized for its outstanding coffee quality and innovative practices. We are proud to be the primary sponsors of the Bean King Champion contest, and our baristas have won global awards for their skill and creativity. These recognitions inspire us to continue pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in the world of coffee.
